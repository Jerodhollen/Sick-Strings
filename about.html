ABOUT US

Our Mission

"Our mission at Sick String Guitars is to unite you with the guitar of your dreams. It doesn't matter if you are just diving in for the for the first time, you've been rocking out for years, or anything in between‐ we're confident that we can help you find exactly what you're looking for."

We know that finding the right guitar can be a process, so don't feel rushed or overwhelmed during your visit. We want everybody to feel welcome here at the shop, so take as much time as you need! The right guitar will choose you, not the other way around. So feel free to pick up, plug in, and start shredding until you find the one. Our dream is to make sure our fans are satisfied after leaving the show‐ but we don't mind playing encores! If you get home and it doesn't feel right, bring that thing back in! We offer a 30-day, full-refund-guarantee to ensure you're new guitar doesn't leave you singing the blues.

Our Staff

We have hand selected the friendliest, most experienced, and passionate guitarists in the Pacific Northwest to work at our shop! This amazing group of people eat, sleep, and breathe all things guitar. Some people say they are crazy for doing so... and well, they probably are‐ but we love 'em for it! Seriously though, these guys and gals have accrued an overflowing wealth of knowledge over the years and they are here to impart that knowledge unto you, in service of the Rock God's. Come on in and rock out with the band!

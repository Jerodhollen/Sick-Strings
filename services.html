LESSONS

Beginners

If you are brand new and unsure how to start learning to play, this is the class for you. Our expert staff will have you strumming away within days by discovering the anatomy of the guitar, learning major chords and scales, learning how to read notes and tablature, and much, much more!

Intermediate

This is a fantastic class for someone who has learned as much as they can on their own or in another class and want to break through to the next level. Here we hone in the fundamentals and mechanics of your playing, enhance your strengths, and strengthen your weaknesses.

Advanced

Fair warning... This class is very intense and designed for people who have been playing for years. Here you will be fully immersed into a world of musical theory, song-writing, and production. It sounds fun and easy, and, it is! But only if you know what you're doing... your skills will be put to the test.
